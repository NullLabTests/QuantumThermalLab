# REVIEWER COVER NOTE

## What this package is

This package is a conditional validation framework, not a validated experimental result.

It describes a proposed gated 10 mK nitrogen-vacancy (NV) / helium-surface
sensing architecture, organised as 63 explicit decision gates whose statuses
are computed by a deterministic simulation. Every gate that cannot be
satisfied without in-system measurement is labelled BLOCKED, CONDITIONAL,
UNKNOWN, or DERIVED_CHECK — never PASS. The package exists so that the gating
logic can be inspected, run, and disagreed with.

## What this package is not

- It is not a validated system.
- It is not a proof of feasibility.
- It does not claim a breakthrough.
- It does not claim simultaneous LCVD growth and 10 mK sensing.
- It does not claim that any single gate has been physically PASSed.
- It is not a complete source audit. The included source audit is
  REPRESENTATIVE_ONLY.
- It is not a manuscript ready for publication. Bibliography and source
  audit are explicitly marked INCOMPLETE_DRAFT.

## What feedback is requested

A technical sanity check from a reviewer with relevant background in:
NV magnetometry, cryogenic vacuum, helium surface physics, ultrafast laser
heating in dielectrics, or millikelvin instrumentation. Specifically:

1. Whether the τ_c ≥ 292 µs canonical requirement is physically plausible
   for the proposed surface state.
2. Whether shallow NV ensembles can retain usable ODMR contrast at 10 mK
   under this architecture.
3. Whether the cryogenic pulsed molecular beam plus mode-switched dosing
   architecture is credible.
4. Identification of the blocked gate most likely to kill the architecture.
5. Identification of the cheapest first experiment that could falsify it.

A list of suggested questions is in `REVIEWER_QUESTIONS.md`. A list of the
first experiments to attempt is in `FIRST_VALIDATION_EXPERIMENTS.md`.

## What the blocked gates mean

A BLOCKED gate is a check whose prerequisite hardware is not installed or
whose prerequisite measurement has not been performed. BLOCKED is not a
failure of physics; it is an unevaluable state. A CONDITIONAL gate is one
whose underlying physics inequality is satisfied under stated assumptions,
but where no in-system measurement supports the assumption. No CONDITIONAL
gate may become PASS without a real measurement on the actual hardware.
